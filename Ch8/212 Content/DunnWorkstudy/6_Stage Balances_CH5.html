4#

Overall mass balance: $\( V_2 = L_1 + D \)$

For the more volatile component: $\( V_2 y_2 = L_1 x_1 + D x_D \)$

Energy balance: $\( V_2 H_2 + Q_c = L_1 h_1 + D h_D \)$

Where: $\( h_1 = h_1(x_1)\)\( \)\(H_2 = H_2(y_2)\)\( \)\( x_1 \text{and} y_1 \text{are related by equilibrium on stage 1.} \)$

Six equations Unknowns:#

\[ L_1, V_2, y_2, x_1, H_2, h_1\]

Solve these and move to next stage.

5#

Overall mass balance: $\( V_3 = L_2 + D \)$

For the more volatile component: $\( V_3 y_3 = L_2 x_2 + D x_D \)$

Energy balance: $\( V_3 H_3 + Q_c = L_2 h_2 + D h_D \)$

Where: $\( h_2 = h_2(x_2)\)\( \)\( H_3 = H_3(y_3)\)\( \)\( x_2 \text{and} y_2 \text{are related by equilibrium on stage 2.} \)$

Six equations Unknowns:#

\[ L_2, V_2, y_3, x_2, H_3, h_2\]

Solve these and move to next stage.

6#

Overall mass balance: $\( V_{j+1} = L_j + D \)$

For the more volatile component: $\( V_{j+1} y_{j+1} = L_j x_j + D x_D \)$

Energy balance: $\( V_{j+1} H_{j+1} + Q_c = L_j h_j + D h_D \)$

Where: $\( h_j = h_j(x_j)\)\( \)\( H_{j+1} = H_{j+1}(y_{j+1})\)\( \)\( x_j \text{and} y_j \text{are related by equilibrium on stage j.} \)$

Six equations Unknowns:#

\[ L_j, V_{j+1}, y_{j+1}, x_j, H_{j+1}, h_j\]

Solve these and move to next stage.

Stripping Section#

Overbars on V and L indicate flows in stripping section

Unknowns#

  • \(\overline{L}_{f}\)

  • \(\overline{V}_{f+1}\)

  • \(x_f\)

  • \(y_{f+1}\)

  • \(H_{f+1}\)

  • \(h_f\)

Equations#

  • \(\overline{V}_{f+1} = \overline{L}_f - B\)

  • \(\overline{V}_{f+1}y_{f+1} = \overline{L}_fx_f - Bx_B\)

  • \(\overline{V}_{f+1}H_{f+1} = \overline{L}_fh_f - Bh_B + Q_R\)

  • \(h_f = h_f(x_f)\)

  • \(H_{f+1} = H_{f+1}(y_{f+1})\)

\(x_f\) and \(y_f\) are related by equilibrium on stage f.

8#

We have solved the top part of the column and know F.

So we can calculate B and \(Q_R\) from column balances

\(x_B\) could be specified in problem statement

\(y_f\) solved from top down calculations

\(\overline{V}_k = L_k - B\)

\(\overline{V}_k y_k = \overline{L}_{k-1} x_{k-1} - B x_B\)

\(\overline{V}_k H_k = \overline{L}_{k-1} x_{k-1} - B x_B + Q_R\)

\(h_{k-1} = h_{k-1}(x_{k-1})\)

\(H_k = H_k(y_k)\)

\(x_{k-1}\) and \(y_{k-1}\) are related by equilibrium on stage k-1.
